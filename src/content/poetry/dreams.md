---
title: "Dreams"
---

DREAMS speak in tongues to imagination,
worrying old sores,
over-painting with bold colors,
shouting an unknown will.

DREAMS carry what the day forgets,
the face of someone gone,
the room you cannot leave,
the word you cannot say.

DREAMS are the other work —
done without asking,
finished before waking,
already written on the body.
