---
title: "Resolve"
---

I put on my resolve
Armor against the enemy,
Possibility.
Mostly it holds
Amidst thrust and parry,
Though sometimes not.

Thank goodness
The rents are rarely seen.

How easy would it be
Running fingers 'round the edges of the tears,
Separating the folds,
Ripping out the seams
To leave me naked
Too late
Afraid.
